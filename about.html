About axis

Axis was born from a simple observation: AI is already part of how designers work, but it’s used without structure. This project investigates where AI adds real value, where human judgment stays decisive, and how to build a hybrid path that joins both.

Meet the designer

María Fraga Alonso

Designer · Author of Axis

María Fraga Alonso is a designer working across brand identity, packaging, editorial and photography. Axis is her Final Degree Project for the Design degree at Universidad Complutense de Madrid (2025–2026), tutored by David Alonso Urbano — an investigation into how designers can integrate AI into their process with method and criteria.
